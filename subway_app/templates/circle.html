Reading Local and Express stops


The diagram on the left is a merged snapshot of a the A and C train path.

Trains that run on the same line will be illustrated this way on the subway map even though they do not stop at the same stops.

The black and white circles are used to show which trains stop at which station.

  • White Dot: both local and express trains stop there
  • Black Dot: only local trains stop
